Hoptech IoT - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

In Short: Hoptech IoT is a mobile client application that connects to your self-hosted ThingsBoard server. We do not collect, store, or process any of your data on our servers. All data remains on your ThingsBoard instance.

1. Information We Collect

Hoptech IoT collects and transmits the following information solely for the purpose of connecting to your ThingsBoard server:

  • Login Credentials: Username and password for your ThingsBoard server authentication
  • Server Address: Your ThingsBoard server URL (iot.hoptech.co.nz or custom)
  • Device Information: Basic device information (model, OS version) for compatibility purposes
  • App Usage Data: Navigation and interaction data within the app to ensure proper functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

All data collected by Hoptech IoT is used exclusively to:

  • Establish and maintain connection to your ThingsBoard server
  • Authenticate your access to your IoT devices and dashboards
  • Display and interact with your ThingsBoard data
  • Ensure app compatibility with your device

3. Data Storage and Security

Important: Hoptech Limited does not operate any backend servers for this application. All your data is:

  • Stored on YOUR ThingsBoard server at iot.hoptech.co.nz (or your custom server)
  • Transmitted directly between the mobile app and your ThingsBoard instance
  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Stored locally on your device using secure Android encryption for credentials
  • Never shared with Hoptech Limited or any third parties

4. Third-Party Services

Hoptech IoT does not integrate with any third-party services except:

  • Your ThingsBoard Server: The app connects directly to your server
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates only

We do not use:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to anyone.

Since we don't collect or store your data on our servers, there is nothing to share. All data remains between:

  • Your mobile device
  • Your ThingsBoard server

6. Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All data is accessible through your ThingsBoard server
  • Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove local credentials. Manage IoT data through your ThingsBoard server
  • Modification: Update data directly in your ThingsBoard instance
  • Export: Export your IoT data using ThingsBoard's built-in tools

7. Data Retention

  • Login credentials: Stored locally on your device until you log out or uninstall the app
  • IoT data: Retained according to your ThingsBoard server configuration
  • App data: Removed when you uninstall the application

8. Children's Privacy

Hoptech IoT is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. This is a business/professional application for IoT device management.

9. International Data Transfers

Data is transmitted between your device and your ThingsBoard server. The location of data storage depends on where you host your ThingsBoard instance. If your server is located in New Zealand (iot.hoptech.co.nz), your data remains in New Zealand.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Notifying users through the app (for significant changes)

11. Your Consent

By using Hoptech IoT, you consent to this privacy policy and agree to its terms.

12. Security Measures

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all network communications
  • Android Keystore for secure credential storage
  • No logging of sensitive information
  • Regular security updates

This privacy policy complies with:

  • New Zealand Privacy Act 2020

Summary

Hoptech IoT is a simple mobile client. Think of it like a web browser for your ThingsBoard server - it just displays and interacts with YOUR data on YOUR server. We don't collect, store, or see any of your information.
